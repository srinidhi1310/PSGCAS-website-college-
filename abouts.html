About PSG College of Arts & Science

A legacy of academic excellence and holistic development.

Our History

PSG College of Arts & Science (PSGCAS), established in 1947 by the PSG & Sons' Charities Trust, has grown to become one of the most prominent institutions for higher education in South India. With a rich heritage spanning over seven decades, PSGCAS has consistently upheld its commitment to academic rigour, research, and community service.

Mission & Vision

Vision: To be a globally recognized institution fostering academic excellence, innovation, and ethical leadership for societal transformation.

Mission: To provide quality education, promote research, and instill values that empower students to be responsible global citizens, equipped with knowledge and skills for diverse challenges.

Why Choose PSGCAS?

  • Academic Excellence: Diverse range of UG & PG programs with a strong curriculum.
  • Experienced Faculty: Highly qualified and dedicated teaching professionals.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Modern labs, well-stocked library, sports complex.
  • Research & Innovation: Emphasis on cutting-edge research and innovation.
  • Placements: Strong industry connections and excellent placement records.
  • Vibrant Campus Life: Numerous clubs, cultural events, and co-curricular activities.
