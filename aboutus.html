About PSGCAS

PSG College of Arts and Science (PSG CAS) is a College of Arts and Sciences in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1947 by G. R. Damodaran. It was ranked 11th among colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2024. [1]

Our Vision

To empower and inspire a drug-free generation by fostering awareness, promoting healthy lifestyles and providing support to individuals affected by substance abuse, ultimately creating a resilient, informed and compassionate community that stands united against the harms of drug addiction.

Our Mission

PSG College of Arts and Science’s mission is to attain educational excellence by facilitating lifelong learning through advanced technology while fostering a dynamic research environment and promoting national unity among students.
